Policy Overview

This document details the cookie usage practices for Styliqueaxo. We are committed to safeguarding your personal data and ensuring transparency regarding the information gathered through our digital services. This policy provides comprehensive information on the specific cookies we utilize, their operational functions, and your available options for managing these preferences.

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are tiny text files placed on your computer or mobile device by websites you visit. Their primary function is to enable a website to remember your device, preferences, or previous activities, thereby improving your interaction with the site and tailoring information to your individual requirements.

Our Use of Cookies

On Styliqueaxo, cookies serve several purposes: enhancing the site's operational capabilities, retaining your chosen settings, and providing insights into user engagement. We classify the cookies we utilize into these groups:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are indispensable for enabling core website features and facilitating seamless navigation.
  • Analytics Cookies: These gather anonymized data on how users interact with our platform, allowing us to gauge and improve site performance.
  • Advertising Cookies: Employed to monitor browsing behavior across different sites, enabling the delivery of advertisements pertinent to your interests.
  • Preference Cookies: These facilitate advanced website features and personal customization options.

Cookies from External Providers

Occasionally, external service providers may deploy cookies on your equipment. Such cookies support various third-party functionalities, including but not limited to analytical services and promotional content. We advise consulting the respective privacy policies of these third parties to comprehend their data handling practices.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

When you initially access Styliqueaxo, you will be presented with options to either consent to or tailor your cookie selections. You retain the ability to modify your cookie configurations at any point via your web browser settings. Be aware that deactivating certain cookies might impact your overall site experience and limit the range of services we are able to offer.

Adherence to Data Protection Regulations

Our adherence to data privacy laws, particularly those applicable in the United States such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where relevant, extends to the information collected through cookies. We are dedicated to implementing leading data protection practices and respecting user privacy rights, including your ability to request access to or deletion of your personal data.

Security Measures for Your Data

We implement robust technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect your personal information, including data gathered via cookies, from unauthorized access, modification, or deletion. While our commitment to data security is paramount, users also bear responsibility for exercising caution with their personal details, especially when disclosing information over the internet.

Retention of Information

Any personal data acquired through cookie mechanisms is stored only for the duration essential to achieve the objectives detailed in this policy, or for a longer period if mandated or permitted by legal provisions. Should you wish to have your data removed, please reach out to us directly.
